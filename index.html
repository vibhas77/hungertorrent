About US

We at HungerTorrent, A Social Food Delivery System meet your food demands like no others do.
Going out to eat? So why not fill others tummies while they fill your pocket?

When You Place Order

We give you an extra amount along with the cost of order of the food placed before you leave the campus.
You get the meal during your trip ( without even deviating from your route! ) and hand it over to us when you are back. You can thank us later.

When You Receive Orders

You need to pay a minimal extra charge along with the cost of food you order so as to compensate for the transportation cost. We collect the money from you and pass the amount to the person getting your meal. As your parcel arrives, we deliver it to you.
